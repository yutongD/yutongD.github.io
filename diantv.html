# Introduction


Diantv is an app-based course-sharing platform that gathers workers to stay connected and informed, advance their careers, and work smarter. Diantv brings together a wide range of high-quality courses in various industries, allowing users to gain knowledge in a digestible format anytime and anywhere. Users also can create courses and deliver professional content.

# Understanding Users



Identifying core audience

Content consumer: People (Professionals, experts, authors, etc.) or organizations who want to share and deliver professional skills and knowledge via knowledge-based content videos
Content producer: People (Newbies, employees, freelancers, students, etc.) who want to acquire knowledge, improve professional skills, and be inspired

Personas



Key Insights


1. Users cannot finish the course in one sitting

2. Too many notifications can cause confusion and bother

3. Discount information is not easy to find

4. Users do not want to miss necessary notifications and feedback

    but they hate to be disturbed too much by notifications




# competitive analysis



I did a competitve analysis of three platforms to explore how information can be better presented and organized with visual balance or hierarchy.




Key Insights


1. Configuration and preference options should be clearer and more accessible

2. Keep things clear and concise under profile pages

3. Notifications should provide enough information to inform about an event and encourage users to take action
