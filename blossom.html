BLOSSOM

A women's period tracking app.

  • Teamwork
  • April 2019 - June 2019

    Pen & paper, Sketch, Online survey builder (Survey Star), Axure RP

Blossom is a team project related to the course Design of User Interface, we were required to collaborate with team members to do user research, then each student completed their final prototype. Blossom is a women's health app mainly for women’s period & ovulation tracking.

THEY ARE TOO PROACTIVE

Many period tracking apps require constant thinking about time tracking as a task, a boring task without any feedback. Open the app, click to start the cycle, open the app again, click to end the cycle. These apps are compete with things that are more important in peoples lives, rather than delight people. So they lose.


DISCUSSING MY PERIOD WITH OTHERS
MAKES ME UNCOMFORTABLE



Commonly, people feel awkward when talking personal stories to others. Personal boundary lacked when people seek for help from acquaintances on some embarrassing symptoms or personal behaviors. In contrast, people tend to speak out freely to strangers online. A person will be more candid when nobody knows who you are and you can say anything without fear.


IT HAS NO DIFFERENT WITH ADDING EVENTS
ON AN ORDINARY CALENDAR

People become more willing to uninstall an app and keep trying new ones than before. Don't expect too much patience from users. What our users need are period analysis & prediction and personal advice generated from period recording. Simply ask users to report daily matters are meaningless. People want a period assistant rather than a tracker.


I KNOW THEY ARE HELPFUL,
BUT ... TOO PROLIX, TOO BORING.



The defining characteristic of the modern reader is impatience. Healthcare videos and articles are helpful, but no one want spend much time on one-hour health care courses after busy work..






Part 3: Persona



DESIGN GOAL

Now we come to the step of translating discoveries above into goals. I asked myself:


How to strengthen the connection with users?
What if the recording period is as easy as clicking on a button?
What if it specified for them, tailoring targeted health plans?
What if it provides you a sense of safe environment to express yourself where nobody knows who you are?


I was determined to create better solutions.


Style

A warm, cheerful interface and graphic design.
Simple and well-organized functional areas.
Easy-to-find period tracking module.


Features

An anonymity community for free talking and querying.
Short videos about women's health that enable watching in fragmented time.
Period recording must be very simple and requires little user input.
Accurate period analysis.


User Flow

Keeping the requirements I distilled from the research in mind, I drew a sitemap to map out the basic layout of the functions I wanted to include in the app and designed the user flow.


FINAL PROTOTYPE



JUST A CLICK
Period tracking consists of a series of buttons for input without typing.




PRIVACY
When the users ask or answer anonymously, the system randomly assigns a nickname to the user, such as "banana".


HEALTHCARE KNOWLEDGE
We provide two forms of healthcare videos. Short videos of 4-5 minutes that enable watching in fragmented time; live videos enable real-time interaction with quick feedback.
