BLOSSOM

A women period tracking App.

Teamwork
  • June 2019 - April 2019

Pen & paper, Sketch, Online survey builder (Survey Star), Axure RP

Blossom is a team project on Design of User Interface, we were required to collaborate with team to do user research, then complete final prototype on my own. Blossom is woman health app mainly for woman period & ovulation tracking.




MY ROLE

In research phase

Designed 10 survey questions with team;
Semi-structured interview; 
Personas and user flows.

In design phase

Completed prototype;
Conducted usability testing.

In planning phase

Facilitated team meetings;
Led whiteboarding.
